Air Quality App

Designing an app that will inform all Melon employees for the quality of the air around them. It will allow users to check the air quality in real time. The app will also provide notifications with some recommendations. It will allow all the employees to receive location-based data for the quality of the air. All other applications do not support notifications, on demand measurements and comparison between the measurements.

This app will show the quality of the air which we all breath. The employees will receive real time benefit to have all the information for their current location.

client: Melon
Scope of work: UX/UI, Mobile Design, App Design, Graphic Design
Development: Melon
Project owner: Melon
