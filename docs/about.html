About those things

Founded by Lyudmil Dachev as a solo operation. Now Three One Four is small collective of creatives and friends based in Sofia, Bulgaria. We use our collective experience to meet the demands of the modern market.

What we do

We can help your company, find it's visual voice. We are hard at work in the field of branding, strategy, identity and digital goodies.
