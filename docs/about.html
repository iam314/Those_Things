Things about us.

Those Things is a creative-driven studio, based in Sofia, Bulgaria. The professional experience we have is based on our design expertise and technical knowledge. Our collaborative nature keeps us curious and we embrace every new challenge with an open heart and passion for finding the right solutions.

Things we do.

The studio is focused on creating effective design solutions, powerful identities, flawless interactions, and inspiring experiences to connect people to brands and organisations.

Services: Identity and Branding, Web Design and Development, Motion Design and Complate Solutions for your Brand.
