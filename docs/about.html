Things about us.

Those Things is a creative-driven studio, based in Sofia, Bulgaria. The professional experience we have is based on our design expertise and technical knowledge. Our collaborative nature keeps us curious and we embrace every new challenge with an open heart and passion for finding the right solutions.

The studio was founded by Lyudmil Dachev and Jivko Yordanov. We can design Things, create Things, tell Things, sell Things, we can offer you Those Things you need for your business to succeed.

Things we do.

The studio is focused on creating effective graphic design solutions, powerful identities, flawless interactions, and inspiring experiences to connect people to brands and organisations.

Our services are in the field of : Discovery, Research, Strategy, Branding, UX/UI Design, Visual Design, Motion Design, Illustration, Prototyping, Animatics, Web Design and Web Development.
