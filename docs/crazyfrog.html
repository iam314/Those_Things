Crazy Frog

Crazy Frog is an online retailer of children's clothes and accessories. We got involved in the project when the company was in its infancy. The project goals were to create a fresh logo and brand identity.

We took the newly created brand assets and apply them to a custom shopify theme. With the magic of some third-party apps, we even made it into a PWA.

client: Crazy Frog
Scope of work: Logo Design, Identity, Graphic Design, Web Design, Custom Shopify Theme, PWA
