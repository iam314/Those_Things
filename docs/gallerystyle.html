Gallery Style

Super stoked about this project. Typically I outsource my back-end development, but this time I went all in. The task was to design and develop a website for an art gallery that provides art classes. The general idea is to choose what you want to paint and sign up for it. For now, the booking is only available on the phone. At a later stage we will add online bookings.
