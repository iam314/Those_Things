Outsider Talks

OUTSIDER TALKS podcast is all about exploring alternative lifestyles and what it means to achieve personal satisfaction and success outside of traditional norms. The show features guests from diverse fields who have chosen to pursue unconventional paths, sharing their stories, challenges, and lessons learned along the way.

We have created a logo that reflects the podcast's focus. A visual identity that embodies the show's message of celebrating those who have chosen to live life on their own terms. The logo features unique typography and bold colors, conveying a sense of creativity and individuality. The symbol included in the logo is indicating that the podcast is a space for conversation, dialogue, and sharing experiences.

client: Four o Four
Scope of work: Logo Design, Identity
