Blade Retail Display and Packaging

We have designed a permanent retail display and packaging for a draught beer machine company.

The display is intended to showcase the company's draught beer machines in a visually appealing and engaging way. The design has taken into account the storage space requirement, making the display an efficient space-saving solution. The packaging is designed to protect the machine during transport and makes it easy for customers to understand the features and benefits of the product.

The sleek and modern design helps both the display and the packaging to stand out in retail environments. This way the company can increase their sales and promote their products more effectively.

client: Zagorka Bulgaria
Scope of work: Graphic Design, 3D Rendering, Packaging, DTP
